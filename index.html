About

Let me introduce myself

I'm Sam, a front-end developer and designer. After working as a graphic designer for a print shop in the Lake Norman area of NC for many years, I became interested in learning what it takes to design and build websites. What started as a simple curiosity led me to gain the knowledge and experience necessary to totally pivot my career. Now I'm a full-time front-end web developer with a passion for creative design that is both visually pleasing and functional.
