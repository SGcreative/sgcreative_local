About Us

M.D. Home Furnishings in Springfield has earned a reputation for providing quality furniture at competitive prices since 1927. We are a full service home furniture store including free in-home design services, free delivery and free financing.


At M.D. Home Furnishings, we carry a complete line up of home furniture including Living Rooms, Dining Rooms, Family Rooms, Home Office, Entertainment, Outdoor, Bedrooms, Youth Bedrooms, Mattresses and even Bedding. We offer a "good, better, best" price range in all of these categories.
